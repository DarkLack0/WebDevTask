Introduction

What is Juggling?

Juggling is the process of tossing and catching the objects (usually balls) with at least 1 always in the air. Usually, it is just a good trick to show friends and relatives.
But for circus actors - it is their life.

Why do you need to juggle?

Before you start juggling, it's good to understand the goal you want to aim for. It is recommended to continue only when there is a definite goal and this applies not only to juggling but also to any other hobby.

Difficulties of juggling

  1. LEVEL 0. Toss 1 ball. Everybody already here.
  2. LEVEL 1. Toss 2 balls. First exercises appear.
  3. LEVEL 2. Simple 3 ball juggling. The real juggling begins.
  4. LEVEL 3. Juggle 2 balls in one hand, 3 ball juggling.
  5. LEVEL 4. Take other objects. For example stones, apples or clubs.
  6. LEVEL 5. Juggle 4 objects. It feels like beginning of madness.
  7. LEVEL 6. Juggle 5 objects. Real professional level.
  8. LEVEL 7. Take more and more different objects. And combine juggling with other skills!
  9. Street Juggler. Show skills to people.
  10. Juggling Performer. Share abilities with pro.

What do you need to reach it?

  1. LEVEL 0. 1 Second is enough to complete.
  2. LEVEL 1. It will take around half of hour.
  3. LEVEL 2. Some hours is what you need.
  4. LEVEL 3. 2 days and you become a juggler.
  5. LEVEL 4. Will be fine in a week.
  6. LEVEL 5. Only after a month this level will be reached.
  7. LEVEL 6. Mmm... 3 months may be enough.
  8. LEVEL 7. After spending a few years, you can get it.
  9. Street Juggler. It will require only 1 life..
  10. Juggling Performer. Anyway, without luck there is no chance.

Strengths and Weaknesses of Juggling

  • You will never forget how to juggle.
  • Improve spatial thinking and hand motility.
  • Possible to combine with other abilities.
  • It never gets boring.
  • Takes time and effort to learn.
  • The equipment is necessary.
  • A small chance to earn a luxurious life with this skill.
