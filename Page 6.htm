Become a juggler

Find a job

To proof your skills, the best option is to find a job. Usually, jugglers can work in the circus or be the juggling instructor.

Circus

Most likely, it will not be easy and it will take a lot of time to join the team. However, after all, success will await you. To find this job you can visit online websites such as 24 Juggler Jobs, Gigs & Auditions but we highly recommend going to a real circus and chatting with their team.

Instructor

Instructor of juggling is a person who teach how to juggle other people. You will need good knowledge about juggling, the ability to teach people of different ages and from different classes. This work is similar to the work of a teacher or trainer and is suitable for calm people who can convey the main aspects of juggling through words and actions.
If you recognize yourself from the lines above, we advise you to visit available jobs.

Street juggler

However, the other option is to become a "street juggler", traveling the world and demonstrating various skills on the street. This is suitable for those who do not consider juggling a duty and prefer freedom.
To become such a person you will need to overcome many obstacles and also stop hoping for a quiet life, because now every day is a new adventure. In addition, such qualities as confidence, eloquence, the ability to improvise are needed, and it would be great to know other tricks besides juggling. The more tricks you know, the higher your chances of success. To get started, take everything you need, go outside and show your abilities. After you realize that people are bored with seeing you, move to another place or city, or country...
