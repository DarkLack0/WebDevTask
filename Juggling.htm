Real Juggling

Level 1

Next step is 2 balls. It is the most important part to learn juggling.

The steps you need to follow:

  1. Take 2 balls (1 ball in each hand)
  2. Toss 1 ball with one hand
  3. Wait until it reaches max height
  4. Toss the 2nd ball with the other hand
  5. Catch the 1st ball
  6. Catch the 2nd ball
  7. Keep practicing!

Do not forget to repeat everything, starting with the other hand.

Level 2

Real juggling is very close. It is the last step to complete the basics.
Now is time to take 1 more ball.

Insruction may help you:

  1. Take 3 balls (2 ball in one hand and 1 in the other)
  2. Toss the 1st ball
  3. Wait until it reaches max height
  4. Toss the 2nd ball
  5. Catch the 1st ball
  6. Wait until 2nd ball reaches max height
  7. Toss ball number 3
  8. Catch the 2nd ball
  9. Wait until 3rd ball reaches max height
  10. Continue juggling

Never forget your non-dominant hand and start with it too.

Theoretically, the instruction is correct, but in practice, the operations of tossing the next ball and catching the previous one occur almost simultaneously. (For example, steps 5, 6 and 7 may have a different order between themselves or be performed together.)
Do not worry! It's part of the practice!

Extra Tips:

  • Pay more attention to hands.
  • Exercise every day (for at least 10 minutes).
  • Use your best efforts while training.
  • And remember, that people are not perfect.
