Introduction

What is Juggling?

Juggling is the process of tossing and catching the objects (usually balls) while at least 1 always in the air. Usually, it is just a good trick to show friends and relatives.
But for circus actors - it is their life.

Why do you need to juggle?

Before you start juggling, it's good to understand the goal you want to aim for. It is recommended to continue only when there is a definite goal and this applies not only to juggling but also to any other hobby.

Difficulties of juggling

  1. Level 0. Toss 1 ball. Everybody already here.
  2. Level 1. Toss 2 ball. First exercises appear.
  3. Level 2. Simple 3 ball juggling. The real juggling begins.
  4. Level 3. Juggle 2 balls in one hand, 3 ball juggling.
  5. Level 4. Take other objects. For example stones, apples, clubs and other.
  6. Level 5. Juggle 4 objects. It feels like beginning of madness.
  7. Level 6. Juggle 5 objects, juggle 3 in one hand. Professional level.
  8. Level 7. Become circus artist. Impossible!

How much time do you need?

It depends on what level do you aim to reach.
For level 2 one week is enough.
To reach level 3 - 2 weeks.
Level 4 will require up to month.
For level 5 - may be 3 months,
Level 6 - around half of year.
To reach level 7 - you will need whole life of practicing.

Juggling Strengths and Weaknesses

  • You will never forget the juggling.
  • Improve spatial thinking and hand motility.
  • Possible to combine with other skills.
  • Easy to show, just grab something and start!
  • It takes time and effort to learn.
  • The equipment is necessary.
  • A small chance to earn a luxurious life with this skill.
