Advanced Tricks

Juggling 2 balls with one hand

The first trick is good to know after basic juggling is juggle 2 balls using only one hand. It will allow you to understand juggling even more. In addtion, it is this move that will open doors for other juggling tricks.

Juggling Options

There are a lot of different juggling tricks because everything anybody can do is a trick.
In most cases, only the trajectory or juggling style changes.
To learn some of them it is recommended to watch video below.

Any objects

Balls are not only objects for juggling, there are also rings and clubs, but let's see even more. It is good to be ready to show your skills in any situation with any objects. To learn this skill, just try juggling with something other than balls. Start with objects that are not too different from balls. Stones or apples are great. Then try to move on to more and more different objects so that at the end you juggle the first thing that comes to hand.
