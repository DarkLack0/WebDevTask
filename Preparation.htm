Preparation

Equipment

Any object can be used for juggling. Balls are recommended for beginners. The best choice will be special balls for juggling or simple tennis balls. In case you do not have them, try to look for something that looks like a ball, weight around 60g and diameter near to 6.6cm.

The default trajectory

By default the trajectory must be like shown below.

Where red and green colours represents balls, purple and black - hands.

However, if the trajectory is different, then you are either a master or clumsy.

Level 0

Everyone starts with 1 ball.

Instruction:
1. Toss ball in the air with one hand and catch with the other.
2. Repeat the action starting with the other hand.
