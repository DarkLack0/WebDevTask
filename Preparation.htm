Preparation

Equipment

Any object can be used for juggling. Balls are recommended for beginners. The best choice will be special balls for juggling or simple tennis balls. In case you do not have them, try to look for something that looks like a ball, weight around 60g and diameter near to 6.6cm. Simple apples can be an excellent choice, if, of course, you don't feel sorry for breaking them on the way to your success. In addition, it is highly recommended to have spare items just in case, 2 or 3 extra items should be enough.

Level 0

Everyone starts with 1 ball!

Instruction:
1. Toss ball in the air with one hand and catch with the other.
2. Repeat the action starting with the other hand.

Animations will help you every time.
